Our Core Objectives

The United Maritime Association is committed to achieving these strategic objectives to empower maritime professionals and enhance industry standards.

1

Promote continual professional development

Enhance the skills and competencies of maritime professionals through continuous training programs, workshops, and certification courses.

Specialized training programs
Industry-recognized certifications
Leadership development
2

Foster maritime industry growth

Foster partnerships between maritime organizations, educational institutions, and government agencies to drive innovation and best practices.

Public-private partnerships
Knowledge sharing platforms
Joint research initiatives
3

Encourage community outreach and awareness

Foster community outreach and raise awareness through active engagement and shared initiatives.

Host local events
Collaborate with community groups
Share updates on social media
4

Support seafarers' welfare

Promote safety standards and improve the welfare of maritime professionals through comprehensive support programs.

Safety training programs
Mental health initiatives
Emergency response systems
5

Maritime education Training and Guidance

Drive digital transformation in the maritime sector by promoting cutting-edge technologies and innovative solutions.

Digital skills training
Emerging technology awareness
Innovation challenges
6

Sustainability Initiatives

Champion environmental stewardship and sustainable practices across all maritime operations and activities.

Green shipping practices
Carbon footprint reduction
Eco-friendly port operations
