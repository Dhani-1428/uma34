Our Offerings

Workshop Topics

Comprehensive training programs covering all aspects of maritime operations

Navigation & Safety

Advanced navigation techniques, collision avoidance, and safety management systems.

  • Electronic chart display systems
  • Radar and ARPA operation
  • Bridge resource management

Marine Engineering

Maintenance, troubleshooting, and repair of marine engines and systems.

  • Diesel engine maintenance
  • Electrical systems troubleshooting
  • Preventive maintenance planning

Emergency Procedures

Comprehensive training on handling emergencies at sea.

  • Firefighting techniques
  • Abandon ship procedures
  • Medical first aid at sea

Regulatory Compliance

Understanding and implementing maritime regulations and standards.

  • SOLAS requirements
  • MARPOL compliance
  • ISM Code implementation

Environmental Protection

Sustainable practices and pollution prevention in maritime operations.

  • Oil spill response
  • Ballast water management
  • Energy efficiency measures

Digitalization & Technology

Modern technologies transforming the maritime industry.

  • E-navigation systems
  • Autonomous shipping concepts
  • Blockchain in maritime logistics
