Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our maritime medical support services.

Response times vary based on location and circumstances, but our emergency team can typically initiate evacuation procedures within 1-2 hours of receiving a request. We maintain relationships with air ambulance services worldwide to ensure the fastest possible response.
