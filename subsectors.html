Detailed Services

Our Comprehensive Offerings

Specialized services tailored to meet the diverse needs of maritime professionals

Training & Development

Enhancing seafarers' skills through specialized training programs in navigation, safety protocols, and emergency procedures.

  • Standardized training programs
  • Industry-specific education
  • Mentorship and coaching

Medical Support

Comprehensive healthcare services including medical fitness certification and mental health support for seafarers.

  • ILO/MLC compliant certifications
  • Occupational health services
  • Mental wellness programs

Job Generation

Creating employment opportunities and connecting seafarers with potential employers across various maritime sectors.

  • Job placement services
  • Career counseling
  • Internship facilitation

Infrastructure Development

Establishing maritime institutions and developing facilities to support education, training, and seafarer welfare.

  • Shipping company offices
  • Training facilities
  • Technology investments

Family Welfare

Supporting families of seafarers through various initiatives including education, healthcare, and community building.

  • Family assistance programs
  • Educational support
  • Community initiatives

Women Empowerment

Promoting women's participation in the maritime sector through skill development and leadership opportunities.

  • Career development programs
  • Leadership training
  • Diversity initiatives
