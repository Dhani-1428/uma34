Our Heritage

Distinguished Maritime Legacy

The United Maritime Association (UMA) was founded in 2024 by a group of passionate mariners with a shared vision to promote excellence, innovation, and sustainability in the maritime industry. Today, we're a thriving community of over 3,000 members, with offices in Kolkata, Patna, Ranchi, and Lucknow dedicated to supporting seafarers and driving industry growth.

Our association represents the convergence of tradition and innovation, creating a strong, inclusive community of maritime professionals committed to advancing the industry.

"Join us in our mission to empower seafarers, elevate the industry, and promote sustainability. Together, we can make a difference!"

Our Mission

Fostering Maritime Excellence

Comprehensive Maritime Professional Development

To foster maritime professional development, provide valuable services, and promote the traditions and standards of the maritime sector in India. We are committed to creating an ecosystem where every seafarer can achieve their highest potential while contributing to the nation's maritime supremacy.

Our Services

Comprehensive Maritime Solutions

We offer a complete range of services designed to support maritime professionals throughout their careers

Training & Education

Educational seminars, workshops, and comprehensive training programs to enhance seafarers' skills and knowledge in navigation, safety protocols, and emergency procedures.

Medical Support

Comprehensive healthcare services including ILO medical examinations, mental health support, counseling, and wellness programs for seafarers.

Job Placement

Career counseling, job search support, and connections with potential employers across various maritime sectors.

Family Welfare

Support programs for seafarers' families including education assistance, healthcare support, and community building initiatives.

Women Empowerment

Programs to promote women's participation in the maritime sector, leadership development, and career advancement opportunities.

Publications

e-Magazines, industry reports, and knowledge sharing platforms to keep members informed about maritime trends and best practices.

Our Objectives

Pillars of Maritime Excellence

Strategic objectives that guide our every endeavor and shape the future of maritime professionals

Professional Development

Promote continual professional development through standardized training programs, mentorship, and skill enhancement initiatives.

Industry Growth

Foster maritime industry growth through infrastructure development, technology adoption, and strategic partnerships.

Education Excellence

Deliver world-class maritime education and training that aligns with international standards and industry requirements.

Seafarer Welfare

Support the holistic well-being of seafarers through healthcare, mental wellness programs, and family support services.

Community Outreach

Encourage community engagement and raise awareness about the maritime industry's vital role in global commerce.

Digital Innovation

Promote digitalization and innovation in maritime operations to enhance efficiency, safety, and sustainability.
